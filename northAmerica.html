South America
Continent in the western and southern parts of the earth consisting of 12 countries. The largest and most populous country is Brazil, while the smallest is Suriname. The continent is known for its Amazon rainforest, Andes Mountains, and rich culture from the Inca civilization to Spanish and Portuguese colonial influences

FunFact!

    North America

  • Third largest continent
  • Home to the largest freshwater lake
  • Home to the Grand Canyon
  • Home to the world's largest economy
  • Diverse cultures and languages

    South America

  • Largest continent by land area
  • Largest country by land area
  • Largest country by population
  • Home to the Amazon rainforest
  • Home to the most populous city in the world
